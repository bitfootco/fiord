Everything your finance team needs

Clearledger brings together the tools and workflows that modern finance teams rely on every day.

Expense tracking

Capture receipts automatically, categorize spend in real time, and enforce policy limits before charges hit your books.

Approval workflows

Route purchase orders, invoices, and reimbursements through configurable approval chains. Set thresholds by department, vendor, or amount.

Cash flow forecasting

Predict your runway with AI-powered projections. Model scenarios, stress-test assumptions, and present board-ready forecasts in minutes.

Corporate cards

Issue virtual and physical cards with built-in spend limits. Transactions sync automatically with your GL — no more manual reconciliation.

Automated reporting

Generate P&L statements, balance sheets, and custom reports on a schedule. Share with stakeholders via PDF, CSV, or direct dashboard links.

ERP integration

Sync bidirectionally with NetSuite, QuickBooks, Xero, and Sage. Push journal entries and pull vendor data without manual imports.
