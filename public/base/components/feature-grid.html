Everything your team needs to move fast

Meridian brings together the tools and insights that high-performing product teams rely on every day.

Real-time dashboards

Monitor key metrics as they happen. Live event streams, rolling windows, and auto-refreshing charts keep your team in sync without manual exports.

Cohort analysis

Segment users by any combination of attributes and track their behavior over time. Understand which acquisition channels drive long-term retention.

Funnel visualization

Map every step of your user journey and identify exactly where drop-off occurs. Compare funnels across segments, time periods, and experiment variants.

Intelligent alerts

Set threshold-based or anomaly-detection alerts on any metric. Get notified in Slack, email, or PagerDuty before a problem escalates.

SQL explorer

Query your raw event data directly with a built-in SQL editor. Save queries, share results with your team, and schedule automated reports.

Data warehouse sync

Push clean, modeled data to Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift on a schedule you control. No ETL pipelines to maintain.
